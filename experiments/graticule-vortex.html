What's happening

It starts as a swirl of curved lines radiating from the corner. As you scroll it spins and draws inward, and each line takes on a role: five arcs close into the outline, three curl into the meridians, five straighten into the latitudes — the graticule assembling out of chaos.

Keep scrolling — it resolves at the end.

Why it matters

The most literal expression of the brand idea: a diffuse, spinning field of signal pulled into one precise, legible globe. Order out of noise.

How it behaves

Each line is a path sampled at many points; every point interpolates from its spiral position to its target shape, while the whole set rotates a turn and settles. Heavier than the other effects — it redraws every path each frame.
