What's happening

At the top, heavy soft-edged lines with no defined edge. As you scroll, each line sharpens into focus, thins, and spins down into the corner to take its place as a hatch chord — one after another, until the disc is engraved.

Keep scrolling — they resolve in sequence.

Why it matters

Out of focus to in focus is the reader's own experience of a brief: a blur of noise resolving into something sharp and usable.

How it behaves

Each line eases from a thick, blurred, horizontal bar to a thin, sharp chord — blur to zero, a spin of about half a turn, and a drop into the corner — staggered so they focus one at a time. The outline settles at the end.
